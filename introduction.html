Introduction

Access to safe drinking water is essential for health and well-being. Despite progress, billions lack reliable sources, leading to health risks and social inequalities. This presentation explores the importance of safe water, challenges faced, and potential solutions. The access to safe drinking water is a fundamental human right. Empowering communities to ensure access to clean water at the village level is crucial for public health and sustainable development. This presentation will explore strategies to enhance water access and promote community empowerment.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenges

  • Limited access to clean water sources
  • Contamination from industrial/agricultural runoff
  • Lack of awareness about waterborne diseases
  • Poor infrastructure and maintenance

Solutions

  • Community education and awareness programs
  • Regular water quality testing
  • Investment in water infrastructure
  • Promotion of household purification methods
