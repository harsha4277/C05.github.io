Week 2: Supply Facility Survey

• In Day 1 we visited to Nellore to make a observation on water supply in that area. We observed that most of the people depends on Bore water, filter water, bottle water, tap water.

•

In Day 2 we went to sachivalayam and we discussed about the user’s water problem’s that we find out in the previous week.

•

In Day 3 we visited the same locality and we communicated with the people about the access of the water. we given the awareness about water problem of the user’s.

•

In Day 4 Again, we visited the same street and we continued to give the awareness about the water problems of the user’s. We given solution to the people to file the complaint in the sachivalayam for not getting sufficient water.

•

In Day 5 We visited the other areas in Nellore and we created awareness about user’s problem. We created awareness about how inconsistent access of the water impact on daily life hygiene and water diseases.

•

In Day 6 We visited same area and we survey the people about water diseases. We given awareness about the water diseases like fever, dry cough, typhoid, skin problems etc… to avoid this problems we have given awareness about the purification methods like filteration, boiling, distillation methods.

Key Learnings

  • Identified main sources of water supply in the area.
  • Observed maintenance and operational challenges.
  • Noted community feedback on water availability.
